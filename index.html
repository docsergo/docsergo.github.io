About Me

I am an Associate Professor of English in the Department of English at Kennesaw State University. Born and raised in southern New Jersey to two immigrants from rural Portugal, I received a B.A. in English from Rowan University, a M.A. in English (Rhetoric & Compostion) from Marshall University, and a Ph.D. in Rhetorics, Communication, and Information Design from  Clemson University.
